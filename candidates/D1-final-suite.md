headline runs, three are excluded solely because

shipped; only commit-pinned source metadata and hashes remain.

boundary recorded on July 11. The surviving records do **not** prove they were a literal
prefix of the frozen S5 popularity array, so this artifact makes no such claim. Before
any headline execution, the July 12 continuation walked the frozen 628-row S5 pool in
recorded popularity order, assigned every row through index 445 one deterministic
first-failing disposition, and stopped immediately at the eighth additional strict
admit. This produces the fixed **30-skill** target without result-dependent substitution.

The boundary is machine checked by `experiments/manifests/rq1-skills.draft.json`,
`candidates/D1-continuation-audit.json`, and `skillrace.d1_selection`. All 33 public
records have commit-pinned source metadata and local byte hashes in
`experiments/manifests/third-party-skills.json`; 25 redistributable upstream license
texts are embedded in the artifact.
